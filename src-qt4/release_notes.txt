iqr 2.x release notes
---------------------

Version 2.x is the latest version of iqr, and based on Qt 4.5. 
At the moment this is release is still alpha.

Backwards compatibility
.......................
Warning: Due to the change from Qt3 to Qt4, some of the modules might not work anymore.

Updates
.......
The most visible change in the GUI is the logic of re-routing connections, the ability
to assign custom colors to processes and groups, and the new filter dialog for the system
browser.
